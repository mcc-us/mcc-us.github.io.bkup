## DEAR = CLIP  

- **DEAR = CLIP**: helps differentiate between debits and credits.   

| Debit + | Credit (-)|
|:--------|----------:|
| Drawings, Expenses, Assets, Receivables ||
   
| Debit (-) | Credit +|
|:----------|--------:|
|| Capital, Liabilities, Income, Payables |
