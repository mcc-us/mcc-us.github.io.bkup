3 Common Financial Ratios   
 
1. **Current Ratio** = Current Assets / Current Liabilities   
   
   - *Ratio > 1.5 is good*   
<br>

1. **Debt Ratio** = Total Liabilities / Total Assets 
   
   - *Present as %*    
<br>

1. **Equity Ratio** = Total S.E. / Total Assets   
   
   - *Present as %*   
   - Equity ratio = 1 - Debt ratio
